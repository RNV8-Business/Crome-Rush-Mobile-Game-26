CHROME RUSH – IPHONE WEB VERSION

START
Mac: Double-click STARTEN.html.
iPhone: Publish this complete folder, open its web address in Safari and choose
Share > Add to Home Screen.

CONTROLS
The character jumps automatically. Drag horizontally to land on platforms.
Falling below the screen ends the run. The top-left Home button returns to the
menu and the Pause button opens the pause screen.

PROGRESS
- Gold Coins and BLACK COINS are saved locally in the browser.
- Gold Coins buy regular skins; every purchasable skin costs 300 Gold Coins.
- BLACK COINS buy the regular vehicle ladder in its fixed order.
- Challenge rewards are claimed from SHOP > UNLOCKABLE.
- COLLECTION contains every owned Skin and Car and allows loadout switching.
- Skin Collection sorting: DATE or SCORE. Car Collection sorting: DATE or BOOST.
- CHESTNUT has its own multi-part level unlock progress and payment.
- Every rival stores a separate highscore; every skin and loadout also tracks its
  best distance for challenge progress.

CURRENT CONTENT
- 50 Skins total: 10 regular and 40 challenge Skins.
- 49 Cars total: 21 regular and 28 challenge Cars.
- The 25 newly added challenge Cars use separate side-view Shop art and top-view
  Game art. Their exact boosts and challenge requirements are shown in-game.
- Celebrity challenge Skins use darker silver cards.
- Claimed challenge rewards keep a green border and permanent check mark.
- GOAT remains the final gold completion reward: collect every Car and every Skin.

GAMEPLAY
- BIG DILF and CHESTNUT use distinct level rules, speeds and rewards.
- Cars, shoes, platforms, hazards, Hennessy effects, milestones, particles,
  animations, pause and Game Over results are all active.
- Progress, purchases, selections and highscores are stored with localStorage on
  the current device and browser.
